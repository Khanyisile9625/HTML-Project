Yacht

This is the yacht page.

Did you know? Yachts are often associated with luxury and recreation, and can be powered by sails or engines!

  • Types of yachts: Sailing yachts, motor yachts, superyachts, and catamarans.
  • Fun fact: The word "yacht" comes from the Dutch word "jacht," meaning "hunt."
  • Modern uses: Leisure cruising, racing, parties, and long-distance travel.
