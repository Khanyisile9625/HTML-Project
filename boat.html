Boat

This is the boat page.

Did you know? Boats have been used for thousands of years for travel, trade, and exploration across rivers, lakes, and oceans!

  • Types of boats: Sailboats, motorboats, canoes, kayaks, and more.
  • Fun fact: The oldest known boat is the Pesse canoe, dating back to between 8040 BCE and 7510 BCE.
  • Modern uses: Recreation, fishing, transport, and sports.
